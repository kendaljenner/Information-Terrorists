## 3. 385idéal Label Connection

### Label Structure

**385idéal (also 385i):**
- **Location:** Frankfurt am Main, Germany
- **Founded:** 2008 (evolved from 385Beatz, 2003)
- **Cooperation:** Universal Urban (major label)
- **Distribution:** Universal Music Group

**Name Origin:**
- "385" = Postal code of Bornheim district (60385)
- "idéal" = reference to ideal/aspiration
- Many founders from Bornheim area

### Key Personnel

**Founders:**
- Celo (rapper)
- Abdi (rapper)
- Michael "Syn" Lukaschyk (manager)

**Notable Artists:**
