- Focus: Indie games and occasional FPS titles

**Social Media Expansion:**

### Podcast Venture (2018)

**October 2018:** Launched "Laut und Bunt" (Loud and Colorful) podcast with fellow influencer AnniTheDuck
- **Format:** Paid subscription podcast
- **Outcome:** Discontinued after 1.5 months due to shitstorm/criticism

## The KuchenTV Conflict

### Timeline

**February 3, 2023 - Ongoing:**
- KuchenTV began featuring Shurjoka in increasing numbers of videos

**Scale of Conflict:**
By August 2024:
- Over 280 videos on KuchenTV's main YouTube channel about Shurjoka
- Additional videos on secondary channels and Twitch
- Approximately 1/3 of KuchenTV's content featured Shurjoka

### Legal Actions

**Defamation Charges:**
- Shurjoka filed charges against KuchenTV for defamation (Verleumdung)
- Following months of mutual hostilities in videos and streams
- Other streamers (like Gronkh) drawn into conflict due to shitstorms

**Twitch Response:**
- **December 2023:** KuchenTV's Twitch account suspended indefinitely
- **Reason:** "Harassment of others due to personal trauma"
- Specific incident: Stream containing insulting statements about Shurjoka
- Deutschlandfunk excerpt: KuchenTV stated explicit sexual comments about "breaking" Shurjoka

**Legal Victory for KuchenTV:**
- KuchenTV filed lawsuit against Twitch suspension
- **Outcome:** LG Braunschweig issued preliminary injunction forcing Twitch to reactivate account

### NWO Troll Network Attacks (2024)

**Süddeutsche Zeitung Report (November 2024):**

**Network Identification:**
- Telegram group "Shurjoka Game" founded March 2024
- Created by members of "NWO network"
- NWO = Network of trolls primarily in D-A-CH region (Germany-Austria-Switzerland)

**NWO Network Activities Documented:**
- Doxing
- Swattings
- Contracted killing (in one case)
- Bomb threats

**Specific Attacks on Shurjoka:**
- **June 2025 (Der Standard/Paper Trail Media):**
  - Swatting targeting family member in Graz
  - Trolls claimed to have kidnapped man and his daughter (thought to be Shurjoka's relatives)
  - Ransom demand sent to Shurjoka as "Christmas present"
- Received threat emails
- Supporter of Shurjoka also victim of separate swatting

### Tobias Huch Legal Case (2024-2025)

**Background:**
- Activist Tobias Huch had disputes with Shurjoka on Twitch and YouTube
- **May 2024:** LG Frankfurt am Main initially prohibited Huch from calling Shurjoka "Hatefluencerin"
- Granted other requests from Shurjoka partially

**Appeal Outcome (July 2025):**
- **OLG Frankfurt am Main ruled:**
  - "Hatefluencerin" designation is acceptable opinion expression
  - Statements that Shurjoka sued Huch "because she didn't like what he said about her" are acceptable opinions
  - "Spreads hate as content" and "misogynistic behavior" are acceptable opinions
  
- **Not Allowed:**
  - Unproven factual claims that Shurjoka "incites hatred day in, day out"
  - Claims she sexually harasses others
  - Claims her business model is spreading hate and fake news
