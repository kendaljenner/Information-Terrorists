## Background

### The "Phantom" of AfD

Tom Rohrböck is a mysterious political consultant who operates behind the scenes of the Alternative for Germany (AfD) party. He has been described as a "phantom" by German media due to his lack of official party membership or formal function, yet his significant influence within the party.

### Key Characteristics

- **Never a Member:** Never officially a member of the AfD party
- **No Official Function:** Never held an official position within the party structure
- **Significant Influence:** Despite lack of formal role, exercises considerable behind-the-scenes influence
- **Financial Resources:** Demonstrated ability to fund activities and travel

## Documented Activities

### November 2017: Luxury Hotel Meeting

**Location:** Five-star luxury hotel at the foot of the Alps (with infinity pool and mountain views)

**Timeline:**
- November 17, 2017: Entered hotel lobby with blonde female companion
- Duration: Two-day stay
- Payment: Credit card receipts show Rohrböck paid for both rooms, drinks, and meals at the hotel's top restaurant

**Context:**
- This occurred in the autumn when AfD first entered the Bundestag (German federal parliament)
- The meeting demonstrates access to financial resources and luxury accommodations

### February 2018: Media Coverage

A laudatory article appeared in the small online publication "Hessen-Depesche" praising one of the new AfD members of parliament. The article coincided with Rohrböck's activities and the AfD's new presence in the Bundestag.

### June 2021: AfD Investigation

**Development:** The AfD federal board decided to investigate Rohrböck's role in the party.

**Background:**
- Following media reports about his activities
- Questions about his unofficial influence
- Investigation initiated during telephone conference on Monday morning
