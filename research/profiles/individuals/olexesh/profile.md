## Background

### Early Life

Olexesh spent his first six years of life in Kiev (Kyiv), Ukraine:
- **Father:** Belarusian (emigrated to USA when Olexesh was 2 years old)
- **Mother:** Ukrainian

**Migration to Germany:**
- Age 6: Moved to Germany with his mother
- First lived in Kelkheim (Taunus)
- Later moved to Darmstadt-Kranichstein where he spent most of his youth

### Education
- Attended special needs school (Sonderschule)
- Discovered passion for rap music during school years

## Musical Career

### Discovery and Early Work (2011-2012)

**2011:**
Frankfurt rapper Celo (of Celo & Abdi duo) discovered Olexesh through his self-produced music videos on YouTube.

**First Video:** "Super 6" (first self-produced music video released on YouTube)

**Record Deal:**
Signed with 385idéal label and began working on debut mixtape.

**December 2, 2012:**
Released debut mixtape "Authentic Athletic"
- **Format:** Free internet download
- **Content:** Nearly 30 tracks
- **Style:** Westcoast hip-hop instrumentals
- **Downloads:** Over 200,000 (as of 2012)

**Breakthrough Features:**
- Featured on Haftbefehl's "Blockplatin" album (deluxe edition)
- Collaboration with Mosh36 on "BZ" mixtape
- Appearances on "Halt die Fresse" online format

### Album Discography

#### 1. Nu eta da (2014)
**Release:** March 7, 2014
- Debut studio album
- Guest features: Gzuz, Fard, Eko Fresh, Karate Andi, Celo & Abdi
- Hit single: "Purple Haze" (became scene hit)

#### 2. Masta (2015)
**Release:** March 27, 2015
- Guest features: Sido, Veysel, Hanybal, Celo & Abdi

**Mixtape:** Straßencocktail (September 25, 2015)
- Second mixtape
- Included G-Funk style tracks alongside classic rap

#### 3. Makadam (2016)
**Release:** October 21, 2016
- **Chart Position:** #2 German album charts
- Singles: "Weyauu," "Geboren in der Großstadt"
- Guest features: Xatar, Haftbefehl, Gzuz, label colleagues

#### 4. Rolexesh (2018)
**Release:** March 2, 2018
- **Chart Position:** #1 German album charts, #1 Austrian charts
- Major commercial breakthrough

**Mixtape:** Radioaktiv (March 2, 2018)
- Single: "Magisch"
- **Chart Position:** #1 German singles chart
- **Certification:** Platinum (400,000+ units sold)

**Crossover Collaboration:**
July 6, 2018: Released "Wir 2 immer 1" with schlager singer Vanessa Mai
- Deliberately crossed genre boundaries between rap and schlager
