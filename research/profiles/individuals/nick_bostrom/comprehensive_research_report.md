## 4. Simulation Argument

### The Trilemma

Bostrom's simulation argument posits that at least ONE of these is very likely true:

1. **Extinction Hypothesis:** "The fraction of human-level civilizations that reach a posthuman stage is very close to zero"
   - We go extinct before achieving advanced technology

2. **Interest Hypothesis:** "The fraction of posthuman civilizations that are interested in running ancestor-simulations is very close to zero"
   - Advanced civilizations don't want to simulate ancestors

3. **Simulation Hypothesis:** "The fraction of all people with our kind of experiences that are living in a simulation is very close to one"
   - **We are almost certainly living in a simulation**

### Implications

- If #3 is true, our reality is simulated
- Raises questions about the nature of consciousness, free will, and existence

  - Deepfake/simulation capabilities
  - Reality manipulation
  - Virtual environments (ILM StageCraft)
