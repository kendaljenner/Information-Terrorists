## 3. Próspera Characteristics

### Charter City Model

**Governance:**
- ZEDE (Zone for Employment and Economic Development)
- High degree of autonomy from Honduran government
- Independent administrative systems
- Custom legal and fiscal framework
- 9-member council (5 elected, 4 appointed by Honduras Próspera Inc.)
- Honduras Próspera Inc. has veto power (2/3 majority required)

**Economic Features:**
- 1% tax on business revenue
- 5% tax on wages
- 2.5% sales tax (2025 data: also 5% personal income tax)
- Bitcoin recognized as legal tender
- Custom civil and commercial codes
- Businesses can select foreign jurisdiction regulations

**Physical Infrastructure:**
- Armed security at entry gates
- "Temporary touristic access permit" required
- Construction began 2021
- Patrik Schumacher-designed initial buildings
- "The Circular Factory" (robotic construction, 2025)

### Economic Activities

**Conferences & Events:**
- 2024 biotech startup events ($1.5M venture capital investments)
- Theme: "Make death optional"
- February 2025: "Crypto cities summit"

**Real Estate:**
- Primary revenue source
- Land sales and development
- 88% revenue to city, 12% to Honduran development trust
