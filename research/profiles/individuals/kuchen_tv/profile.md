## Major Controversies and Legal Issues

### Volksverhetzung (Incitement to Hatred) Convictions

#### First Conviction - 2016
- **Charge:** Volksverhetzung (incitement to hatred)
- **Context:** Posted a joke on Twitter about a 12-year-old girl
- **Defense:** Lawyer stated joke was regrettable, not justifiable as dark humor, would not be repeated today
- **Outcome:** Accepted penalty order due to lack of financial resources to fight it
- **Status:** Officially has criminal record for Volksverhetzung

#### Second Conviction - 2018/2025
- **Initial Charge:** Volksverhetzung
- **Context:** Retold an antisemitic joke in a stream, rated it "not so bad"
- **Additional:** Insulted a student (who received civil courage award) with "nasty swear word"
- **Initial Sentence:** 32,000 Euro fine (2018)
- **Appeal:** Reduced to 20,000 Euro by Oberlandesgericht Rostock
- **Constitutional Complaint:** Rejected by Bundesverfassungsgericht (Federal Constitutional Court) in August 2025
- **Status:** Conviction final and binding

### Nazi Symbolism Charges (2024-2025)

#### December 2024 Incident
- **Allegation:** Said "Sieg Heil" in a livestream on December 22, 2024
- **Defense:** Claimed he stumbled over the word "Qualität" (quality)
- **Outcome:** Freed in court hearing (November 2025) - acquitted

#### December 2025 Conviction
- **Court:** Amtsgericht Braunschweig (Braunschweig District Court)
- **Date:** December 10, 2025
- **Charge:** Use of symbols of unconstitutional and terrorist organizations
- **Details:** Monted Hitler's head onto a bodybuilder's body in YouTube video
- **Sentence:** 70 daily rates at 100 Euro each (7,000 Euro total)
- **Context:** Would have been legal if distancing from Hitler had been clear, but judge could not determine this
- **Status:** Not yet legally binding; defense announced appeal (Sprungrevision)

## The Shurjoka Conflict

### Timeline of Conflict

**February 3, 2023 - Ongoing:**
KuchenTV began increasingly featuring Shurjoka in his videos.

**Scale:**
By August 2024, had published over 280 videos about Shurjoka on his main YouTube channel, plus additional videos on secondary channels and Twitch.

**Frequency:**
According to Zeit Online, approximately **one-third** of KuchenTV's videos feature Shurjoka.

### Stated Reasons
Heldt justifies the focus by claiming:
- Shurjoka is part of an intolerant "woke bubble"
- Shurjoka plays the victim role
- Shurjoka mentions him in many of her videos and streams

**Characterization:**
Described the conflict as "YouTube beef" that is financially profitable for both sides.

### December 2023: Twitch Ban

**Reason:** "Harassment of others due to personal trauma" and "psychological pressure" on Shurjoka

**Legal Response:**
- Filed lawsuit against Twitch suspension
- Court could not sufficiently justify why suspension was warranted
- **Outcome:** LG Braunschweig issued preliminary injunction forcing Twitch to reactivate his account

### Escalation and Troll Networks

**November 2024 - Süddeutsche Zeitung Report:**
- Troll groups from "NWO network" became aware of Shurjoka through the conflict
- Telegram group "Shurjoka Game" founded (March 2024) by NWO network members
- Trolls organized hate campaigns against Shurjoka

**Attacks Documented:**
- Doxing
- Swatting
- False accusations
- Both KuchenTV and Shurjoka became victims of organized trolling

**Note:** KuchenTV became an unwitting keyword generator for hate campaigns but also became a target himself.

### ZDF Magazin Royale Coverage (September 2024)

**Episode:** "Gaming-Communitys: Der letzte unpolitische Ort auf Erden"

**Content:**
- Showed excerpts from KuchenTV's founded Steam channel "Kuchenrepublik Deutschlands Rapecake"
- Channel contained rape fantasies
- KuchenTV claimed not to have known about the threads shown
- Stated he changed the channel name
