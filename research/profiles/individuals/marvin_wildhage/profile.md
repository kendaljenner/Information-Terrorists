## Background

### Early Career

Wildhage began his media career at age 14, conducting interviews with celebrities for his YouTube channel "startalking." Between 2011 and 2019, he interviewed prominent figures including:
- Klaas Heufer-Umlauf
- Udo Lindenberg
- Olli Schulz

Since 2013, he has regularly conducted interviews with German rappers for "TV Strassensound."

### Education

- **2016:** Abitur (high school diploma) at Gymnasium am Silberkamp in Peine
- **2016-2018:** Two-year journalism training at Axel-Springer-Akademie in Berlin
- Parallel to training: Increasing on-camera work as entertainer

## Career Evolution

### Transition to Pranks and Undercover Work (2019+)

**2019 - First Major Prank:**
Published video titled "Interview with Dieter Bohlen" - a fake interview using a Dieter Bohlen impersonator. The prank sparked media debate about deception in content creation.

**Same Year - Security Breaches:**
- Gained unauthorized access to training grounds of Hannover 96 and Hertha BSC football clubs
- Actions went viral and resulted in invitations from both clubs

### Major Controversies and Legal Issues

#### 2020 - Fake Doctorate Scandal

**Incident:**
- Used forged documents to have a doctorate title entered into his identity card
- Goal: Expose negligence of Berlin civil registration offices

**Legal Consequences:**
- Reported for indirect false certification (mittelbare Falschbeurkundung)
- **January 14, 2022:** Convicted by Amtsgericht Tiergarten (Tiergarten District Court) and fined
- Sparked public discussion about bureaucratic carelessness

#### 2021 - Joyn Production "Achtung Aaron"

Exposed gullibility of influencers with fake product "Hydro Hype" cream. Later projects included:
- Fake film "A Hole"
- Fake esotericism shop
- False pregnancy scoop about Laura Müller

**Purpose:** Criticize credulous media and online stars

#### 2021 - Vaccine Passport Investigation

Publicly criticized slow progress on digital vaccine passport. During research, demonstrated how simple it was to acquire a forged vaccine passport.

#### 2024 - European Football Championship Security Breach

**June 2024 - Major Stunt:**

Wildhage infiltrated the opening ceremony of Euro 2024 by:
- Adopting false identity as mascot "Albärt"
- Moving freely on the playing field before the match began
- Only discovered when he couldn't find his way to the dressing room

**Impact:**
- Raised serious questions about stadium security concepts
- UEFA filed charges against Wildhage
- Refused to comply with injunction to prevent publication of footage
- **Status (May 2025):** Legal proceedings still ongoing

#### 2025 - Federal Cross of Merit Scandal

**April 2025:**
Ordered an authentic Federal Cross of Merit (Bundesverdienstkreuz) under a false name to expose deficiencies in the award allocation process.

**Statement:** "It feels so wrong to hold this Federal Cross of Merit in my hands"

**Purpose:** Highlight problems with Germany's highest honor allocation system
