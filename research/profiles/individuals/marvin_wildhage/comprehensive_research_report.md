## 2. Detailed Incident Analysis

### 2.1 Fake Doctorate Scandal (2020-2022)

#### Timeline

| Date | Event | Source |
|------|-------|--------|
| **July 15, 2020** | Applied for new ID at Bürgeramt Wedding (Osloer Straße), Berlin | B.Z. Berlin, Berliner Kurier |
| **August 21, 2020** | New Personalausweis issued with "Dr." title | Court documents, B.Z. |
| **September 2020** | Published YouTube video "Doktor erschleichen!" (~1M views) | YouTube, B.Z. |
| **December 2021** | Court case initiated | Gentside, Multiple sources |
| **January 14, 2022** | Trial at Amtsgericht Tiergarten, Berlin | B.Z., Ingame.de |
| **January 2022** | Convicted and fined | Multiple sources |

#### Methodology

**Documents Used:**
- Fake doctoral certificate (Promotionsurkunde)
- Claimed: "Dr. der Naturwissenschaften" (Doctor of Natural Sciences)
- Grade: "cum laude" (with honors)
- Fictional University: "K.T. Gutenberg Universität Mainz" (Johannes Gutenberg Universität exists, "K.T." version does not)
- Two fake professor signatures

**Process:**
1. Created fake certificate using "ein bisschen Photoshop" (a bit of Photoshop)
2. Paid €28.80 for new Personalausweis
3. Applied at Bürgeramt Wedding
4. No verification performed by staff
5. Three weeks later: Official ID with "Dr." title

**Duration of Fraud:**
- Doctor title valid on ID for **5 days**
- Wildhage claimed he "canceled" it after proving his point

#### Legal Outcome

**Charges:**
1. Urkundenfälschung (Document forgery)
2. Mittelbare Falschbeurkundung (Indirect false certification)

**Sentence:**
- **40 daily fines at €80 each = €3,200 total** (B.Z., Ingame.de)
- Alternative calculation mentioned: €3,200 or 80 daily fines at €40 (some sources vary)

**Court Proceedings Details:**
- **Judge's comment:** "I didn't expect it to work" (Wildhage's statement in court)
- Wildhage appeared on crutches ("Kreuzbandriss, Fußball" - cruciate ligament tear, football)
- Two Bürgeramt employees testified
- Employees claimed they "couldn't remember the case"
- **No internal consequences at Bürgeramt**
- No registry of universities/study programs introduced after incident

**Wildhage's Defense:**
- Goal: Expose overburdened Berlin authorities
- Journalistic intent: "Missstände aufzeigen" (show abuses)
- Reaction: "Schade" (shame) that video had no impact on procedures
- Released music video "5 TAGE DOKTOR" capitalizing on incident

**Secondary Consequences:**
- Attracted **7 lawsuits** total (Gentside)
- Risked potential arrest warrant
- Potential for "Präzedenzfall" (precedent case) for journalists
