## EVIDENCE INTEGRITY PROTOCOL

### Chain of Custody

1. **Source URL** - Original location documented
2. **Archive URL** - Wayback Machine or local copy
3. **Date Archived** - Timestamp
4. **Verification Hash** - MD5/SHA256 for files
5. **Researcher** - Who archived
6. **Cross-Reference** - Related evidence links

### Verification Standards

- **Tier 1 sources:** Require 2+ independent corroborations
- **Tier 2 sources:** Document single-source limitations
- **Tier 3 sources:** Flag for verification, use with caution
- **Social media:** Screenshot with timestamp, URL, context
- **Video:** Download original, preserve metadata
